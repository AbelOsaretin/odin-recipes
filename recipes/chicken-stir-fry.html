Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This quick and easy chicken stir fry is packed with flavor and colorful vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken and vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add garlic and ginger, saut√© for 1 minute.
  5. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  6. Pour in soy sauce and stir to combine.
  7. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
