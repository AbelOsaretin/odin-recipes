Tomato Pie

Description

This savory pie is filled with fresh tomatoes, herbs, and cheese, all baked in a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pie crust and place it in a pie dish.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with salt, pepper, and chopped basil.
  4. Spread the ricotta mixture over the pie crust.
  5. Layer sliced tomatoes on top of the ricotta.
  6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the tomatoes.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
